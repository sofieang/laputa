Exporting Statistics

When running a batch simulation, Laputa records the individual e-values of all inquirers at any time, and for any trial. This data, or a subset of it, can be saved in .ods (OpenDocument Spreadsheet) format.

For each dimension of the spreadsheet, a single variable can be picked. Checking the Average (or Final) button will make Laputa average over that variable, or in case it is time, pick the last moment. Thus averaging over trials will result in a spreadsheet containing the average e-value per inquirer and time, over all the trials made.
